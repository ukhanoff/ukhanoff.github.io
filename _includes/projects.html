KEYPR In-Room tablet

  • Improving hotel guest staying quality

As a part of KAOS (KEYPR Android OS) team I'm working on the In-Room tablet solution for the hospitality. It's a mix of Android framework customisations and Google Enterprise API to provide professional device management and great guest staying experience. With tablet hotel guests can order in-room dining, run 3d party Apps (Netflix, HULU, WSJ etc.), make reservations, engage with staff and more.

REMi-KEYPR Smart Mirror

  • Next gen hospitality experience

It's a huge 42+ inches, interactive and a highly customizable Android-based Smart Mirror. With my team we have been integrate REMi Mirror to the KEYPR hospitality system.
